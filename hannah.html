My personal leadership philosophy

My leadership philosophy prioritizes connection and communication. I think that without forming a proper, empathetic connection with people, one cannot lead. Knowing and understanding people would help a leader to prioritize the proper things.

Inspirational leader to me

An inspirational leader to me would have to be my Aunt Connie, she has always perservered and has supported me in everything I do. She constantly tells me to value myself and my leadership skills no matter what. Over the years she has guided me in both my education, and my career. She has helped me craft cover letters, resumes and emails to ensure I fufill my true potential even if most of the time I don't have the energy or confidence to do so <3

My Leadership style + Strengths and Weaknesses

I take more of a a ffiliative leadership style. I prefer to form connections and foster a positive, friendly environment. This also means I tend not to lead at all, I very rarely take a leadership role and am more of a support beam of a worker more than anything. I am more than happy to let someone else take the lead, as long as they're being kind while doing so! Some of my leadership strengths are that I am empathetic and understanding of ones circumstances and perspectives, people often tell me I am quiet and kind. Although to speak on negatives, sometimes I can be too kind, to my own detriment. I often let people walk all over me.
